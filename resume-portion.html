Chief Technology Officer

Mr. Rodney Morris is Chief Technology Officer at Graham Technologies (Graham) where he is responsible for driving the company's technology vision, which is to continuously enhance the innovation on behalf of Graham's customers at a global scale. Prior to joining Graham, Mr. Morris worked as a Sr. Software Developer at Leidos where he was a principal developer and team lead for classified and unclassified customer engagements in search of scalability and robustness of mission-critical enterprise computing systems. Mr. Morris holds a M.S. from The George Washington University, a B.S. from Coppin State University, and is cleared up to TS/SCI with full scope polygraph.
